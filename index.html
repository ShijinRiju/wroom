About MyWroom

Drive Success, Forge Connections, and Achieve Growth with MyWroom.

MyWroom is a professional network of ambitious business owners who meet weekly to collaborate on optimizing and advancing their businesses and personal lives. Members can expand their customer base and increase sales through high-quality referrals and exclusive MyWroom mechanisms. They become part of a community committed to mutual support and societal impact. Additionally, MyWroom provides valuable training and sessions to promote continuous personal development, enhancing both professional skills and personal well-being.

CVO

Liju R Nair is the founder and CVO of MyWroom. After completing 21 years’ central government service in Delhi, he began to actively pursue his lifelong passion for entrepreneurship. He started an interior contracting firm in 2013 and forayed into the arena of gene profiling in 2018.

During his entrepreneurial pursuits, he became part of various business referral clubs and realised their power to fuel the multi-dimensional growth of business owners. Attending multiple training sessions and coming in contact with many successful business owners made him realise the still untapped potential of business referral clubs, prompting him to establish MyWroom as an organisation. Since the first chapter of MyWroom launched in February 2021, MyWroom now has a strong presence across multiple cities in India.
